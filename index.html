Jackfruit

Jackfruit is a breadfruit with a length of 20 centimeters to 1 meter. Weight reaches 35 kilograms. The Indian breadfruit is famous for the largest edible fruits, which are attached directly to the trunk with the help of strong pedicels. Jackfruit ripens up to 8 months. The green pulp of unripe fruits is fried and stewed like vegetables. When ripe, the pulp acquires a bright yellow color, sugary sweet, slightly oily taste. The aroma of the fresh fruit is reminiscent of a melon. more..

Papaya

Papaya is used in cooking and medicine, and the stems and bark of trees are used as raw materials for ropes. Ripe papaya fruits are usually eaten raw, peeled and seeds, added to fruit salads and desserts. In salads, papaya goes well with parmesan and other hard cheeses. Due to its high pectin content, papaya can be used to make jellies. more..
